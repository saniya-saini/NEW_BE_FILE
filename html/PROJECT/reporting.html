Why Report?

Your reports help us identify problematic drivers, improve bus conditions, and make public transportation safer for everyone. Every report contributes to building a better transit system.

What Happens Next?

Reports are reviewed by our team within 24-48 hours and forwarded to transport authorities for investigation and appropriate action. You'll receive updates if you provide contact information.
